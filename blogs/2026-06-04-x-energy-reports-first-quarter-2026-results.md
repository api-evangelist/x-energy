X-Energy announced its first quarter 2026 financial results as a newly public company, reporting revenues and grant income of $43.4 million, nearly double the prior year. The company achieved significant regulatory milestones including receiving a Part 70 fuel fabrication license from the NRC and obtaining environmental clearance for Dow's advanced nuclear project in Texas. Major business development highlights included signed supply agreements and memoranda of understanding with strategic partners to support commercial deployment of its Xe-100 small modular reactors.
